YanbuHub | Check Out What You've Helped Us Build

Hello everyone, Thank you very much for joining us during the first two months of this test. We are deeply grateful to everyone who has booked personal sessions and attended the live ones. We've learned a great deal about supporting creators within an African ecosystem. Here are our key insights: Content creation is challenging, and achieving stability in creative roles, such as working for agencies or as ghostwriters for brands, better positions individuals to launch independent careers. With these findings, we've decided to dedicate the final month of this testing phase to skills development and support. We are developing actual products to aid anyone in thriving in a creative environment, whether as a contributor or while building a following, independently or as part of a team. We will also offer consultancy services to help people secure roles and connect them with opportunities. Being part of this hub should guide anyone with a keen interest toward a fruitful journey as a content creator, independently or as a contributor. On that note, we have redesigned our final test, which will launch this March. In the meantime, we're eager to engage with those already in roles or looking to enter the content creation space. Take advantage of live sessions, group conversations, and DMs to ask questions and share challenges you're facing in the workplace, and we will ensure that we can help you succeed. Thank you very much for your continued support. We look forward to providing more assistance and growing the hub together. Thank you.
